About Potlucks

As stated before potlucks are magic. There's an inexplicable french expression about a gathering of people eating the food of their neighbors. Sharing is caring. The right side of history. A road to a better tomorrow. Walking on sunshine. And a list of other euphemisms that you could easily imagine being printed on a picture of a sunset or cute kitten. (Can I haz cheezburber? LOL!!!!) We digress...

We love food- we love people. So naturally we love people's food.

Bottom line: We. Love. Potlucks.
